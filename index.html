About the Artist

About Me

Creating a sound without fixed boundaries

I am a bassist, producer, and creative technologist based in Los Angeles. My musical background includes electric bass, upright bass, studio recording, electronic production, and live performance.

My work brings together the energy of house and techno, the freedom of jazz, and the atmosphere of ambient music. I am especially interested in using technology to make live electronic performances feel more expressive and human.

  • Electric and upright bass
  • Electronic music production
  • Live looping and MIDI performance
  • Studio recording and audio engineering
